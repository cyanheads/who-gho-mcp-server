# who-gho-mcp-server

Global health indicators and disease surveillance data via the WHO Global Health Observatory API.

## Data source

- **WHO GHO OData API** — global health indicators, disease surveillance, mortality/morbidity
- **Auth**: None required
- **Rate limits**: Generous, public OData endpoint

## Why it earns its keep

Directly complements CDC (US-only) with worldwide health data for 194 WHO member states. Life expectancy, disease prevalence, immunization coverage, health system capacity, SDG health targets. Genuine public good for health researchers, journalists, and policy analysis.

## Target users

- Health researchers comparing countries
- Journalists covering global health topics
- Policy analysts working on health systems
- Agents combining with CDC for US-detail + WHO for global context
- Development professionals tracking health SDGs

## Scope

- Read-only
- Indicator search and metadata (1000+ indicators)
- Country-level data queries with year ranges
- Dimension/filter browsing (region, income group, sex, age group)
- GHO theme/category navigation
- Country profiles
